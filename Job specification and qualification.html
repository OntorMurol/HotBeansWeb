JOB SPECIFICATION AND QUALIFICATION

DESIRED QUALIFICATIONS WHAT WE WANT IN A CANDIDATE

If you want to become a trainee web developer, you will need to have some background knowledge in certain areas. 

 This could include: 

 A GCSE or BTEC equivalent in Computer Science 

A GCSE or BTEC equivalent in Art or Design 

An award at Level 2 for Creating a Website 

A certificate at Level 2 for Interactive Media 

A diploma at Level 2 or 3 in Creative iMedia 

A specialist diploma at Level 3 in Information Technology 

A diploma at Level 3 in IT User Skills

You should love web development and be interested in the latest trends and technologies. 

You should pay close attention to detail and be dedicated to writing clean, efficient, and well-documented code. 

You should have strong problem-solving and analytical skills. 

You should be able to work both independently and in a team environment. 

You should have excellent communication and people skills. 

You should have a positive attitude and be eager to learn and develop alongside the company. 

You should have a portfolio of previous work that showcases your skills and experience in web development.
